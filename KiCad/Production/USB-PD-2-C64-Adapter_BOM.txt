BOM for USB-PD-2-C64-Adapter - grouped by Baugruppe
Matches schematic USB-PD-2-C64-Adapter.kicad_sch and JLC-ready CSV USB-PD-2-C64-Adapter_BOM.csv.
Baugruppen: USB-PD Eingang, 5VDC, 9VAC. The 9VAC power-stage has BOM-ready start values, but LC filter values, VREF/current regulation, THD, no-load voltage, and thermal behavior remain measurement-critical before PCB release.
Project constraints: the adapter must use USB-C PD 15V/3A as its only energy source; transformer or second-supply paths are not project options. C64 DIN-7 pinout is documented in .github/copilot-instructions.md. The future 9VAC branch must be disabled when no 5V load is drawn and must stay at or below 10.3VAC RMS no-load.
